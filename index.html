Jeffrey Patrick Kinney ( Fort Washington , Maryland , February 19 , 1971 ), better known as Jeff Kinney , is the creator of the Diary of a Wimpy Kid series . He also created the Poptropica website. In 1998, three years after moving to New England , he decided to write the book Diary of a Wimpy Kid . Originally, his book was a digital comic that she had worked on for around eight years to come up with the idea. Kinney says that in creating Greg she drew on his childhood, with a slight exaggeration to make it more comical and expressive. Thus, in 2006, the first stories of Greg were published in physical format.In 2009, with the publication of his third book, Jeffrey was named one of Time Magazine's 100 Most Influential People in the World . As of 2020, Kinney had written fifteen volumes of Diary of a Wimpy Kid and won the Nickelodeon Kid's Choice Awards six times for favorite book.Kinney is also the creator of Poptropica , one of the 50 best websites according to Time.

He lives in Massachusetts, along with his wife and two sons, Will, eleven years old, and Grant, thirteen, who now inspires him to create his books. In his spare time, Kinney practices volleyball and spends time with his family. Together they have a bookstore in Massachusetts, known as "An Unlikely Story".
